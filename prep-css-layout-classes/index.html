Temple of Doom

The second installment in the Indiana Jones franchise released on May 8, 1984, Indiana Jones recues children from a religious cult in India. After arriving in India, Indiana Jones is asked by desperate villagers to find a mystical stone and rescue their children from a Thuggee cult practicing child slavery, black magic, and ritualistic human sacrifice in honor of the goddess Kali.

The Last Crusade

Released on May 24, 1989, The Last Crusade is the third installment in the Indiana Jones franchise. The story begins with a flashback to 13-year-old Indiana Jones stealing a golden crucifix from a group of grave robbers in hopes to donate it to a museum. Years later, Jones runs into the same group of robbers and discovers that his father went missing while searching for the Holy Grail.
